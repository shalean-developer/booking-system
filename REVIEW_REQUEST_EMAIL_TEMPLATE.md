## How to Get Your Google Review Link

1. Go to your Google Business Profile
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy the short link provided
4. Replace `[YOUR_GOOGLE_REVIEW_LINK]` in templates above

**Or create a custom link:**
- Format: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR_BUSINESS_ID]/review`
- You can find your business ID in Google Business Profile settings
