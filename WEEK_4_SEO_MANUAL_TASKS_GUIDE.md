## ‚úÖ Task 2: Set Up Local Citations

### What Are Citations?
Citations are online mentions of your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) on other websites. They help establish your business's legitimacy and improve local SEO.

### Step 1: Prepare Your NAP Information

**Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone):**
```
Name: Shalean Cleaning Services
Address: [Your physical address OR service area: Cape Town, Western Cape, South Africa]
Phone: +27 87 153 5250
Website: https://shalean.co.za
Email: support@shalean.co.za
```

**Important:** Use EXACTLY the same format everywhere!

## ‚úÖ Task 3: Create Review Collection Process

### Step 1: Set Up Review Request System

**After Service Completion Email Template:**

Create an automated email that goes out 24-48 hours after service completion:

**Subject:** "How was your cleaning service? Share your experience!"

**Email Body:**
```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing Shalean Cleaning Services!

We hope you're happy with your cleaning service. Your feedback helps us improve and helps other customers find trusted cleaners.

Would you mind leaving us a quick review?

üìù Review Options:

1. Google Business Profile (Most Helpful!)
   ‚Üí [Direct Link to Google Review]

2. Facebook
   ‚Üí [Link to Facebook Page]

3. Email Review
   ‚Üí Reply to this email with your feedback

Your review helps us serve more customers in Cape Town. Thank you!

Best regards,
The Shalean Team
```

### Step 2: Create Review Request Page on Website

**Create `/review` page with:**

1. **Thank You Message**
   - "Thank you for choosing Shalean!"
   - "Your feedback helps us improve"

2. **Review Options**
   - Google Business Profile button
   - Facebook review button
   - Email review option

3. **Review Guidelines**
   - What to include in reviews
   - How reviews help

### Step 3: In-Person Review Requests

**Train Your Cleaners:**

When cleaners finish a job, they should:
1. Ask: "How was everything today?"
2. If positive: "We'd love if you could leave us a review! It really helps."
3. Provide review card with QR code linking to Google review

**Review Card Template:**

Print small cards with:
- QR code to Google review
- Website URL: shalean.co.za/review
- Phone: +27 87 153 5250
- "Thank you for choosing Shalean!"

### Step 4: Respond to All Reviews

**Response Templates:**

**5-Star Review Response:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled you're happy with our service. We'll make sure [Cleaner Name] sees your kind words. Looking forward to serving you again!
```

**4-Star Review Response:**
```
Thank you, [Name]! We appreciate your feedback. We'd love to earn that 5th star - please let us know how we can improve. We'll be in touch!
```

**3-Star or Lower Review Response:**
```
Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. We take feedback seriously and would like to make this right. Please contact us at support@shalean.co.za or +27 87 153 5250 so we can address your concerns.
```

**Response Best Practices:**
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Be personal and genuine
- Thank reviewers by name
- Address specific concerns
- Don't be defensive

### Step 5: Review Incentive Program (Optional)

**Consider Offering:**
- Small discount for leaving a review
- Entry into monthly prize draw
- Priority booking for reviewers

**Important:** Check Google's guidelines - you can't pay for reviews, but you can thank reviewers.
