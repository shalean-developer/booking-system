## Content Guidelines

### Word Count
- **Minimum:** 800 words per post
- **Target:** 1000-1200 words for comprehensive guides
- **Maximum:** 1500 words (avoid overly long content)

### Heading Structure
- **H1:** One per post (title)
- **H2:** Main sections (3-5 per post)
- **H3:** Subsections within H2 sections
- **H4+:** Use sparingly for nested content

### Internal Linking Strategy
- **Minimum:** 2-3 internal links per post
- **Target:** 4-6 internal links per post
- **Link Types:**
  - Service pages when mentioning services
  - Location pages when mentioning areas
  - Related blog posts
  - Booking page for CTAs

### Image Requirements
- **Featured Image:** 1200x630px (Open Graph size)
- **Content Images:** 800x600px or 1200x800px
- **Alt Text:** Descriptive, keyword-rich
- **Format:** WebP or optimized JPG
- **Example Alt Text:** "Professional deep cleaning service in Sea Point, Cape Town - Shalean Cleaning Services"

### Content Quality Standards
- **Original Content:** All posts must be unique, not duplicated
- **Value-Driven:** Provide actionable tips and insights
- **Location-Specific:** Include Cape Town/local context where relevant
- **Service Integration:** Naturally mention Shalean services
- **Call-to-Action:** Include booking CTA at end of posts

## SEO Best Practices

### Title Tags
- Include primary keyword near the beginning
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include location when relevant
- Example: "Deep Cleaning Guide Cape Town | Shalean Blog"

### Meta Descriptions
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Include call-to-action
- Example: "Complete guide to deep cleaning your Cape Town home. Expert tips, room-by-room checklist, and when to hire professionals. Read more!"

### URL Slugs
- Use descriptive, keyword-rich slugs
- Keep under 60 characters
- Use hyphens, not underscores
- Example: `/blog/deep-cleaning-guide-cape-town`

### Content Structure
- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Include bullet points and numbered lists
- Use bold/italic for emphasis
- Include relevant images every 300-400 words
